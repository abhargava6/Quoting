Looking to book shuttles for a few World Cup games — 4 shuttles per game from different locations

⚠ Gap — vehicle dimensions / venue constraints Need your input

What's happening: the parking passes cap vehicle size (9ft × 18ft), which rules out sprinters/mini buses → SUV only. The system stores seat capacity, not physical dimensions.

Our approach (proceeding): when a client states a size/venue limit, the assistant steers the recommendation to a vehicle that fits and flags the constraint as a note (it doesn't change price).

Question for the team: good enough to handle dimensions/venue access as a steering rule + note, or do we need real vehicle dimensions in the system?

⚠ Gap — event-based pickup timing Need your input

What's happening: there's no flight to anchor on — pickups are driven by the event (kickoff + the ticket access window). Client wants ~1.5h early.

Our approach (proceeding): compute suggested pickups from kickoff − desired-lead − drive time per origin, and present them for confirmation.

Question for the team: is "suggest pickup times from the event window" useful — and what default lead time do you use for stadium events?

✓ Working — quote without exact locations Confirm

What's happening: with locations TBD, we quote on an hourly assumption (5h service + 1h garage-to-garage), exactly as the team did, and firm up locations before booking. (Quotes don't hold availability.)

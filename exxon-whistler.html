Question for the team: is side-by-side scenario comparison the right default for "quote me a couple of ways"?

⚠ Gap — vehicle amenities & venue access Need your input

What's happening: the request needs a coach with a PA system, plus signage; and (later) a full-size coach can't reach the golf course / gondola (narrow driveway, no parking). None of these are modeled in the system.

Our approach (proceeding): capture PA/signage as notes on the quote, and treat venue-access limits as a steering/flag (e.g., drop at the hotel + shuttle, as ops did).

Question for the team: are notes + a flag enough for amenities/access, or should we track which vehicles have PA / which venues have access limits?

✓ Working — garage-to-garage included Confirm

What's happening: the quoted time includes the drive from the Vancouver garage to Whistler (the system's garage-to-garage time) — which is why a 4-hour scenario still carries a 6-hr minimum.
